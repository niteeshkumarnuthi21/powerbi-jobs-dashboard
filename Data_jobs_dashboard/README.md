# Data Jobs listings / Power Bi Dashboard


## Inroduction

- **Job Availability Dashboard (2024)**

This Power BI dashboard provides insights into job availability across different countries for the year 2024 It highlights job roles by **location**, **average** and **hourly salary**, total **job counts**, **trends over time**, and **job ratings by field**, helping users analyze global job market patterns and opportunities.


## Skills Showcased in This Dashboard

- **Data Transformation (ETL) with Power Query**

Performed data extraction, cleaning, and transformation using Power Query to ensure accurate, consistent, and analysis-ready datasets. Applied data shaping techniques such as filtering, merging, and calculated columns.

- **KPI Indicators & Tables**

Designed KPI cards and summary tables to highlight key job market metrics such as total job count, average salary, and hourly wage. Enabled quick performance tracking and high-level decision-making.

- **Core Data Visualization Charts**

Developed essential visualizations including bar charts, line charts, maps, and tables to analyze job availability by country, field, and time. These visuals support trend analysis and comparative insights.

- **Dashboard Design & Layout**

Created a clean, intuitive, and user-friendly dashboard layout focused on clarity and effective storytelling. Applied consistent formatting and visual hierarchy to enhance user experience.

- **Interactive Reporting (Slicers, Buttons & Bookmarks)**

Implemented slicers, buttons, and bookmarks to allow dynamic filtering and seamless navigation across report views. This interactivity enables users to explore job market insights by country, field, and year (2024).

- **Drill-Through Analysis**

Enabled drill-through functionality to allow users to navigate from summary-level insights to detailed job-level analysis. This supports deeper exploration of salary, ratings, and job availability by field and location.
